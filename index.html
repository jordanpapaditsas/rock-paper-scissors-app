Welcome to the Rock Paper and Scissors Deathmatch

Instructions

In this game, the Player must select one option from the following: rock, paper, or scissors images.

The Computer will generate a random option, and the Player or Computer will earn one point for each round won. You need to be choosing rock, paper or scissors every round just by clicking on the images. The first one to reach a score of 5 points will be declared the winner.
In the event of a tie, no points will be awarded.

Press the button to start the game
